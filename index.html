ABOUT ME

Hi there, nice to meet you!

My name is Guillermo (pronounced gee-YAIR-moh) and I'm a Full Stack Developer with a background in scientific research. I started my journey as a developer in 2017, when I completed my first certificate course ("Building Websites from Scratch", at codecademy). Since then I have been learning and applying new technologies to side projects. I am currently enrolled at the Penn Coding Boot Camp, which will conclude around November 2018.


Becoming a developer has been a very exciting and interesting process. I am professional, hard-working and passionate about this new career, and I strive to create beautiful and useful applications.


If you need a website, are looking to hire or if you would like to start a collaboration, please send me an email to gbarila@live.com, or submit your message using the form in the Contact section.
