ABOUT ME

I'm Guillermo, nice to meet you!

My name is Guillermo (pronounced gee-YAIR-moh) and I'm a Full Stack Developer with a background in scientific research. I unofficially started my journey as a developer when I was 12 years old, designing websites for fun using FrontPage. Since then I have been learning and applying new technologies to side projects, and I completed my first certificate course back in June of 2017 ("Building Websites from Scratch", at codecademy). I am currently enrolled at the Penn Coding Boot Camp, which will conclude around November 2018.


Becoming a developer has been a very exciting and interesting process. I absolutely love what I do and cannot wait to apply my knowledge to solve real-life problems. I am professional, hard-working and passionate about this new career, and I strive to create beautiful and useful applications.
